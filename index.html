Welcome to ICCSP

Welcome to the official website of 2018 IEEE 12th International Conference on Communication Systems and Projects (IEEE ICCSP).

The 12th IEEE International Conference on Communication Systems and Projects (IEEE ICCSP)will be held on 18-20 December 2018, in Zhengzhou, China.The conference will feature keynote speeches by distinguished leaders in academia and industry, technical presentations,and invited sessions on all topics that interest researchers in the broad area of communication systems, networks,and applications. It is sponsored by IEEE Communications Society Singapore Chapter,IEEE Vehicular Technology Society Singapore Chapter, Information Engineering University,Henan Institute of Electronics, and IEEE Zhengzhou Section.

We encourage all researchers to submit their best work to IEEE ICCSP 2018, and look forward to welcoming you to Zhengzhou in December 2018.

Important Dates

1.Deadline for abstracts (300 words): 20th July
2.Deadline for full papers: 30th June
3.Deadline for final papers: 10th September
4.Deadline for registration fees: 15th September
5.Conference: 18th-20th December

Publication

All papers, both invited and contributed, will be reviewed by two or three experts from the committees. After a careful reviewing process, all accepted papers of ICGBEM 2018 will be published by E3S Web of Conferences (ISSN:2267-1242) , it will be submitted to EI Compendex, Scopus, Inspec, DOAJ, and CPCI (Web of Science) for indexing.

Submission Guide

1.The submitted papers must not be under consideration elsewhere.
2.Please submit your abstracts(300 words) and papers to the Email lixiangzhi2231@163.com in word or latex format.
3.Please submit the full paper, if presentation and publication are both needed.
4.Please submit the abstract only, if you just want to make presentations.
5.Should you have any questions, please contact us at ICCSP@xd.com.
